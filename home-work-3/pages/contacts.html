How to Reach Us

The best way to schedule a consultation is through our online booking form. For general inquiries, feel free to call us during business hours or send an email anytime. We offer both in-person sessions at our Portland office and virtual coaching sessions via video call for clients anywhere in the world.

Quick Questions

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a complimentary 20-minute discovery session to discuss your goals and see if our coaching is the right fit for you.

Do you accept insurance?

While we do not bill insurance directly, we can provide documentation for you to submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement under wellness benefits.

What should I bring to my first session?

Just bring yourself and an open mind! We will discuss your health history, goals, and current lifestyle during our first meeting. You may want to bring a notebook if you prefer taking handwritten notes.
